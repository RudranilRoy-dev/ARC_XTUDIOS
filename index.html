The Story Behind
arc_xtudios

A creative studio built on the belief that every photograph should move you — long after the moment has passed.

arc_xtudios is built on a passion for storytelling through visuals. Every frame is crafted to capture emotion, detail, and authenticity. We don't just take photographs — we create visual poetry that speaks long after the moment has passed.

We believe photography is not just about images — it's about preserving moments that matter. From the quiet glance between two people to the grandeur of a celebration, we see the magic in every instant.

Based in Kolkata, we travel across India and the world for couples, creators, and brands who value the art of the image.
