How We Work

A Session with arc_xtudios

01

Discovery Call

We begin by listening. A 20-minute conversation to understand your vision, timeline, location, and the feeling you want your images to carry.

02

Creative Planning

We craft a personalised shoot plan — scouting locations, mapping light conditions, and preparing a mood reference that matches your story.

03

The Session

On the day, we guide you naturally — no stiff poses, no forced smiles. Just real moments, beautifully composed and lit with intention.

04

Delivery

Your gallery is hand-edited and delivered within 3–4 weeks via a private online gallery — ready to download, print and treasure forever.

Our Story

The Story Behind
arc_xtudios

A creative studio built on the belief that every photograph should move you — long after the moment has passed.

arc_xtudios is built on a passion for storytelling through visuals. Every frame is crafted to capture emotion, detail, and authenticity.

We believe photography is not just about images — it's about preserving moments that matter. From the quiet glance between two people to the grandeur of a celebration, we see the magic in every instant.

Based in Kolkata, we travel across India and the world for couples, creators, and brands who value the art of the image.
